About Me

Hi! I am Senthil Kumar.

Updated in April 2022

Of my overall 12 years of experience, the last 8 years have in the field of Data Science and NLP. My experience could almost evenly be distributed among three companies - Ford (current firm), LatentView and Beroe. The nature of the work in each of these companies has been different. From performing a Market Research role at Beroe to delivering/managing Data Analytics Services at LatentView. And, from there on to being a data science developer at Ford.

All the above career shifts have been deliberate. In my arguably biased opinion, these different experiences have thankfully moulded me into a better professional than I was previously.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology (alma mater of Late Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam).
